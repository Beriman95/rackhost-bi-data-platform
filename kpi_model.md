Example KPI Governance
Answered Calls

Definition: Successfully handled inbound calls within operating hours

Business Meaning: True customer demand served

Manipulation Risk: Call dropping before connection

Decision Impact: Staffing level calibration

First Response Time

Definition: Time elapsed between ticket creation and first agent response

Business Meaning: Customer perceived responsiveness

Manipulation Risk: Empty placeholder replies

Decision Impact: SLA risk monitoring

SLA Breach Rate

Definition: % of tickets exceeding contractual response/resolution time

Business Meaning: Contractual and reputational risk

Decision Impact: Escalation policy tuning

Strategic vs Operational KPIs

Strategic: SLA Breach Rate, Resolution Time, Demand Growth

Operational: Daily Call Volume, Agent Talk Time, Queue Load
